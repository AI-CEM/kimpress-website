### Service 03 — Webseitenentwicklung & SEO

**Konzept:** Cinematic Before/After Split — Transformation durch Design  
**Format:** 16:9 Landscape  
**Farbpalette Links:** `#D0CFC8`, `#BEBEBE`, `#F5F5F0`  
**Farbpalette Rechts:** `#080808`, `#E63030`, `#FFFFFF`  
**Tool:** Nano Banana 2 / Gemini / Midjourney

#### Prompt V1 — Maximale Kontrolle (empfohlen)

```
Cinematic split editorial photograph. Dark brand transformation
narrative. Photorealistic composite, no illustration, no CGI look.

COMPOSITION: Perfect vertical center split dividing the frame
into two equal halves. Both halves in sharp focus.
No visible seam or border at the split center.

LEFT HALF — BEFORE:
An old silver-grey MacBook or generic laptop on a wooden desk.
Screen shows a cluttered, generic German website — warm beige
tones, messy layout, low contrast. Readable German navigation text:
"Willkommen", "Über uns", "Kontakt", "Unsere Leistungen".
Body copy in German lorem-style blocks. Outdated stock photography
visible in the layout. Environment: neutral office room,
blurred papers and shelves in background.
Lighting: flat, overexposed cool white daylight — deliberately
dull and uninspiring. Color grade: desaturated, slightly washed
out, cold and lifeless.

RIGHT HALF — AFTER:
A sleek matte black monitor (Dell UltraSharp or similar) 
displaying a premium dark-mode website. Bold German headline 
"IHRE MARKE WÄCHST" in heavy white sans-serif. Subheadline 
"Strategie & Webdesign" in crimson red (#E63030). Clean grid 
layout, high contrast, professional hierarchy. Red CTA button 
labeled "Anfragen". Background of monitor screen: pure #080808.
A man's hand and forearm in a black leather jacket sleeve
reaches in from the right side of the frame — fingers extended,
pointing decisively at the monitor screen, as if pulling this
reality into existence. Only the arm and hand are visible,
no face, no torso.
Environment: pure black studio void.
Lighting: dramatic deep red rim light (#E63030) emanating from
behind and around the monitor — painting the surrounding
darkness with crimson. Crushed blacks. High contrast.
Zero ambient fill. Cinematic, intentional, premium.

MOOD: Before and after. Transformation. The visual proof
of what is possible when brand and design converge.

TECHNICAL SPECS:
Camera: Sony α7R V | Lens: 50mm f/2.8 | Photorealistic composite
Color grade LEFT: desaturated, flat, overexposed, cool white
Color grade RIGHT: cinematic dark, crushed blacks, red accent
Aspect ratio: 16:9 landscape. Both screens fully legible.

DO NOT INCLUDE: cartoon style, illustrated arrows, drawn elements,
text overlays outside the screens, visible center seam,
borders or dividers at the split line.
```
